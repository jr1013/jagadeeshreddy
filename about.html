Jagadeesh Reddy is a Filmmaker and multimedia art practitioner based in New Delhi, India. His works expose the human and his relation to the flora, fauna, and inanimate objects surrounded him. Stepping into someone's shoe or placing psychologically in the space of life or the objects is the key essence of his works, Jagadeesh's works deal somewhere in the junction of a belief in the animism and the anthropological construct. His influence was driven towards the fauna while volunteering in a zoo park. Most of his works deal with the historical narratives, mythologies, and their co-relation and co-existence with the flora, fauna surrounde d by humans.
