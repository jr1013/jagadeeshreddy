Visitors from the grounds of the sacred

"A quiet evening" portrays glimpses of an evening from a rooftop. The air is filled with conversations through the radio waves and transport stories from one tower to another. An evening is a time of leisure for many, a time for conversation with loved ones, and a time to gossip with friends. The stories were muted in the end with the advent of the twilight.
