A train to Acclimatization

"A train to acclimatization" is a film of a family in constant flux looking for a house. A family of crows lived on a high-tension power distribution tower. The continuous corona effect and the high winds make the crows live in a harsh environment. A father dreamt to own a house. he says! Let it be in a difficult location. He is in constant search in pursue of a house after he had sold his house due to a financial crisis. The story narrates the father's dream and the crow's nest.
